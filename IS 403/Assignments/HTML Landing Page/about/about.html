About Me

I really like programming.

In 2013, I discovered my passion while working for a small business in Indiana. After I got my first taste of code, I was hooked. Over the next months and years, I wrote a music blog, worked at a rad TV network, and started my own freelance web design business.

These days, I like to say that I am a Rails developer - it is my go-to technology for projects I work on. When I'm not writing rails, though, I am studying Information Systems and Computer Science at BYU, writing some C++, and being evermore interested in JavaScript frameworks and advanced user interaction on the web.

My first love in life was music. I still occasionally post new songs on my Soundcloud and post regularly on the aforementioned Provo Yacht Club.

Feel free to reach out to me anytime via email at asmockler@gmail.com.
